Operator Toolbox

Raw API access: HTML reports, citation fixer, Neo Club JSON, fix-tracker POST, pipeline stage updates, lead inserts, and advanced audit (market / free-tier fields). For everyday URL checks use Quick Audit.

Advanced audit — GET /api/audit

Use for market mode, free package (needs contact fields), or extra parameters. Normal HTTPS sites: Quick Audit.

For package=free, the API also requires contact name, business name, business email, plus URL or industry/location.

Each email links to an interactive 5-question qualifier that buckets respondents into closer-ready personas.

Prospect Hunter

Pick a vertical + state + city → discover the ranking businesses → deep-audit them in bulk → tag winners with owner, years-in-business, notes, and SEO provider attribution. Optional Ahrefs enrichment for backlink and authority signals. Export to CSV when ready to outreach.

State colors (Prospect Hunter model only): green = modeled lower friction for AI-assisted dialing (one-party recording; no extra GeoNeo AI-disclosure flag). yellow = modeled higher friction (all-party consent states and/or states we flag for stricter AI disclosure). This is not legal advice: federal TCPA, FTC, FCC AI-voice rules, and consent still apply everywhere.
