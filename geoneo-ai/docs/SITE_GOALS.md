# Site Goals

## Product Mission

GeoNeo AI helps local businesses improve search visibility across:
- Traditional SEO
- GEO (local/geographic relevance)
- AI-search citation and recommendation readiness

## Primary Business Goals

1. Convert site visitors into qualified leads.
2. Deliver immediate diagnostic value through audits.
3. Guide users into paid tiers (Silver/Gold) with clear upgrade paths.
4. Capture high-quality intake data for follow-up and sales ops.
5. Give internal staff a simple lead dashboard to prioritize outreach.

## User Value Goals

1. Fast visibility score baseline.
2. Clear understanding of risk/opportunity.
3. Practical next-step options based on package.
4. Trustworthy, business-facing recommendations.

## Operational Goals

1. Save complete audit + lead records for each submission.
2. Keep full internal audit data available regardless of customer tier.
3. Preserve compatibility for future email/webhook delivery workflows.

## Success Indicators

- Audit completion rate
- Lead capture completion rate
- Free -> Silver upgrade rate
- Silver -> Gold upgrade rate
- Follow-up contact conversion rate
