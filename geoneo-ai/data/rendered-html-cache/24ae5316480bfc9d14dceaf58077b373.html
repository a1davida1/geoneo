Welcome to Grantlanta Lawn

We offer high-quality landscaping, hardscaping, budget friendly lawn maintenance, installs & cleanups at an affordable price—100% satisfaction guaranteed. Proudly serving all of ATL.

We offer a lot more than just landscaping as well! Gutters, pressure washing, you name it! Our sister company The Southern Shmooze is an additional resource to help point you in the direction of any home project you might need. We also co-run Peace of Mind Recycling for all of your recycling needs offering monthly pickups straight from your porch. Lastly, we are a proud partner of Quiet ATL with our friends in Eco Lawns to offer you an all-electric solution for your landscaping needs in a more eco-friendly approach!
